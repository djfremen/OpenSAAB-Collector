OpenSAAB Collector — Data Collection Consent

The OpenSAAB Collector installs two interception libraries (DLL shims)
into your existing Chipsoft J2534 Pro driver folder:

  - CSTech2Win.dll  (intercepts the Chipsoft D-PDU API used by Tech2Win)
  - j2534_interface.dll  (intercepts the SAE J2534 PassThru API used
    by TrionicCANFlasher and other J2534 clients)

The genuine Chipsoft DLLs are not deleted — they are renamed to
CSTech2Win_real.dll and j2534_interface_real.dll. Our shims forward
every call to the genuine DLLs and write a log of the calls to your
%TEMP% folder.

WHAT'S IN THE LOGS:
  - The CAN-ID, UDS service IDs, and full byte payloads of every
    diagnostic message Tech2Win or your J2534 client sends or receives
  - Hardware timestamps from the Chipsoft adapter
  - Your vehicle's VIN whenever it appears in a diagnostic exchange
  - SecurityAccess seeds and computed keys used to unlock ECMs

WHAT WE DO WITH UPLOADS (if you opt in):
  - Captured logs are sent over HTTPS to https://openSAAB.com/ingest/
  - Logs are stored on the OpenSAAB server, indexed by an anonymous
    per-install GUID (no email, no name, no phone number)
  - Logs are used to build the open-source SAAB protocol catalog at
    https://github.com/djfremen/OpenSAAB
  - We may publish derived YAML command descriptions and decoded DTC
    references; raw logs containing your VIN are NOT redistributed
    publicly without explicit additional consent

YOUR CHOICE:
  [ ] Yes, I consent to upload captured logs.
        — Logs upload automatically when Tech2Win / J2534 client closes.
        — You can pause uploads anytime via the tray icon.
        — You can uninstall at any time and the genuine Chipsoft DLLs
          will be restored byte-for-byte.

  [ ] No, install the Collector for local logging only.
        — Logs still write to %TEMP% (useful for your own debugging).
        — Nothing is sent over the network.
        — You can change your mind later via the tray icon.

By clicking "Next" you confirm:
  - You own or are authorised to operate the vehicle being diagnosed.
  - You understand that captured logs include your vehicle's VIN and
    security access exchanges.
  - You can revoke consent and uninstall the Collector at any time.
